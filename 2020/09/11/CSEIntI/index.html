CSEBA FINAL——this time together

CSEBA June 1st News , Basketball Court, Yuquan Campus

​ The CSEBA playoffs finals continue, the union of class 17-winner of Zijingang and the union of class 15-winner of Yuquan entered the final game.

​ At the beginning of the first quarter, Jiayi Hu joined forces with Yuchen Zhang to make an attacking wave for union of class 17, and Jiacheng He from union of calss 15 responded with a sudden shot. Then the union of class 17 made a 13-2 climax. The union of class 15 were still supported by Jiacheng, and the points difference gradually widened. After the first quarter, the union of class 17 lead the union of class 15 26-12.

​ In the second quarter, the union of class 17 continued their offensive style and continued to get points inside and outside . But Shuyang suddenly broke out in a single quarter and scored 13 points with 3 threes and 1 penalty to help the team to retrieve the rhythm. The union of class 15 continued to pressure the penalty area and hit a 10-3 spurt to reduce the score gap to single digits. After the second quarter, the union of class 17 lead the union of class 15 48-40.

​ During the second half game, the union of class 15 blasted an 8-0 start to equalize the score, but after that the team’s offensive attack misfired, only scored 3 points in nearly 6 minutes, the union of class 17 took the opportunity to return a wave of 15-3 to extend the lead to 15 points. Fortunately, at the end of the quarter, the union of calss 15 once again apporach the point gap to 10 points through free throws.

​ In the last quarter of the game, the union of class 15 were still unable to stop Yucheng’s strong scoring. The union of class 15 who couldn’t attack and defend did not have too many ways to get points, and the game lost suspense to garbage time. Finally the score came to 96-85.

​ ”They are on the brink of elimination.”JingWang said to the reporter, “And now Jiayi and Yucheng have to try to recover from a dismal three quarters in former Zijingang Conference semifinal and hang on to the energy of a fourth-quarter surge that proved there was still signs of life coming from a seemingly flattened bunch.”

​ Jiayi and Yucheng have to convince each other, and their supporting cast, that there is still a chance that this bubble odyssey isn’t over.

​ “The conversation is, number one, take care of the play in front of you,” Jiayi said. “It’s obviously win or go home, so you have to be able to make sure you come out and have that mindset. And it’s one game at a time, especially here down in the bubble.“

​ “It’s a different scenario than any other playoffs, so to be able to take our time and make sure we take advantage of our size and our speed. We have to do it if we want to win. We just have to come back to what gives us the best chance of winning. And that conversation starts tonight and tomorrow and hopefully, we can take care of business the next year.”

Dreamtalker Detector

​ It’s been a long time before Yuman relealized that it’s Jiayi’s somniloquy he faces in the midnight.

​ “It’s pretty loud and scaring”,Yuman said.”I have to wake up and then have difficulty falling in slumber again.”

​ “Somniloquy is a parasomnia that refers to talking aloud while sleep. This parasomnia usually happens during transitory arousals from NREM sleep. The recording of these parasomnias could be useful to help in the diagnosis of certain psychological pathologies given that they can reflect a state of anxiety or some behaviors which could be identified as psychological diagnosis criteria. “

​ In order to find out how the somniloquy come into being Yuman went through a lot of books. He found out the sleep talking is defined by both stages and severity:

Stages 1 and 2: these stages, the sleep talker isn’t in as deep of sleep as stages 3 and 4, and their speech is easier to understand. A sleep talker in stages 1 or 2 can have entire conversations that make sense.

Stages 3 and 4: The sleep talker is in a deeper sleep, and their speech is usually harder to understand. It may sound like moaning or gibberish.

Sleep talk severity is determined by how frequently it occurs:

Mild:Sleep talk happens less than once a month.

Moderate:Sleep talk occurs once a week, but not every night. The talking doesn’t interfere much with the sleep of other people in the room.

Severe: Sleep talking happens every night and may interfere with the sleep of other people in the room.

​ Pic: the-four-stage-of-sleep

​ Sleep talking can happen to anyone at any time, but it appears to be more common in children and men. There may also be a genetic link to sleep talking. So if you have parents or other family members who talked a lot in their sleep, you may be at risk too. Likewise, if you talk in your sleep and you have children, you may notice that your children talk in their sleep too.The sleep talker isn’t aware that they’re talking and won’t remember it the next day

​ “Well, I don’t quite agree”,Jiayi said.”I can remember what I said in the dream, especially when I shouted out loudly, I would tell myself ‘I am dreaming and the just shout may wake my roommate up’, but even that recognization I would still keep on dreaming, still a distance to wakeness.”

​ As the material contracts with Jiayi’s mind, Jiayi and Yuman decide to build a detector to record the voice to verify the test. They use Ubuntu Operating System and Python and a small microphone to get all things done-a mobile platform which only records sleep-talking (excluding other noises or sounds) and analyze them to identify the main words in the voice, allowing making a transcription of the conversation, is presented.
